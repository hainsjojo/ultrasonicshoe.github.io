How Ultrasonic Sensor HC-SR04 works?

HC-SR04 sends continuous ultrasonic sound waves through the transmitter and receives the bounced back waves through the Receiver.
This Sensor is connect to the Arduino UNO board which receives the signals and trigger the Buzzer and the Vibrator connected to the Stick.


The stick also contains a GPS with TalkBack which helps the Blind person to navigate.

A Tracker is attached to the stick for the family members of the blind person to keep track of the person.
